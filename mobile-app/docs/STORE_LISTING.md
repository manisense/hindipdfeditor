## Full description

Edit Hindi and English text in any PDF — scanned forms, letters, and digital documents — right on your Android phone.

**Tap to edit like Canva**  
Open a PDF, tap the text you want to change, and type. On-device OCR finds text automatically. No account required for core editing.

**Works offline**  
Open, edit, mask, and export PDFs without internet. Your documents stay on your device.

**Hindi & Devanagari**  
Proper live shaping for Hindi text while you type. Exported PDFs use embedded Noto Devanagari fonts.

**Optional AI enhancement**  
For difficult scans, optionally use AI OCR through our secure service. Translate detected text from Hindi to English or English to Hindi without entering an API key. Both are opt-in only.

**Export & share**  
Save a new PDF with your changes and share it to WhatsApp, email, or any PDF viewer. Your original file is never overwritten.

---

Free to use. No ads. Built for students, clerks, and anyone who needs to fix a Hindi PDF in a hurry.
