Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 July 2026. This policy explains what Hindi PDF Editor processes on your phone and what happens when you choose optional AI enhancement.

Summary

Hindi PDF Editor is a local-first Android app. You pick PDFs from your device, edit text, and export a new PDF. We do not operate developer servers that receive or store your documents.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, sell, or use personal data on our own servers. The app has no advertising SDK, no analytics SDK, and no account system.

Website Analytics

This website may use Google Analytics to understand aggregate website traffic after a Google Analytics measurement ID is configured. Google Analytics may process technical information such as page views, browser/device details, approximate location, and referral source. This does not change the Android app's local-first behavior.

Data Processed On Your Device

The following data stays on your device unless you choose to export or share a file:

  • PDF files you open through the Android document picker.
  • Text edits, masks, positions, and page state used while editing.
  • Rasterized page images used for preview, editing, export, and on-device OCR.
  • Your Gemini API key, if you use Enhance with AI, stored in Android encrypted storage.

Optional Enhance With AI

If you tap Enhance with AI and provide a Google Gemini API key, the current page image is sent to Google's Gemini API for text detection. This is optional. You can use the core app without it.

When AI enhancement is used, processing is governed by Google's applicable API terms and privacy practices. Hindi PDF Editor does not receive or store your API key or documents on any developer-operated server.

Permissions

Permission Reason
Internet Only for optional Enhance with AI requests to Gemini when you choose it.
Vibrate Standard Android feedback behavior if used by the system or keyboard.

The app uses Android's system document picker and does not request broad storage access on modern Android devices.

Children's Privacy

Hindi PDF Editor is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Data Retention

Document data is controlled by you on your device. Uninstalling the app removes app-private storage. Exported PDFs remain wherever you saved or shared them.

Changes

We may update this policy. The in-app privacy policy link and this page will point to the latest version.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to support@hindipdfeditor.com.
